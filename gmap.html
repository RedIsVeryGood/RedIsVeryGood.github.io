Welcome to the map management page.

Here you have to log in with google and allow access to Google Drive in order to store your maps across sessions.

In addition to this, 4koneko acts more as a quaver web edition (tailored to me, but still) than a seperate game, which is why it only accepts .qp map files found on the quavergame.com website.

Fear not, all you need to do is download them from there (steam account required) and upload them to a folder in your google drive called "4koneko maps".

They will show up here and you can safely go to the map selector and start playing.

Before this please, log in to google. (this site's source code is public, so nothing malicious is going on in the background)
